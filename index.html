Intro & Summary

Imagine for a moment you’re with your friends online, in Sandbox, Decentraland, or somewhere similar, and you all decide to visit your favorite virtual distillery; Shmidt Spirits. You all order cocktails at the bar, hang out, and chat. Before you leave you buy a bottle of whiskey. This bottle of whiskey isn’t just another NFT you can show off to your friends this is more. This bottle of whiskey has utility, it represents a real bottle, in a real distillery, ready for pickup or delivery right into your hands. More than that, it’s an access pass to events, tours, merch, and potentially so much more. c2h5oh is teaming up with Shmidt Spirits Distillery to bring this vision to life. Join us, follow us, and become a part of the experience.

TLDR - What is c2h5oh?

C2h5oh is the intersection of IRL, Crypto, and the craft spirits industry. We want to develop the platform and infrastructure that brings the craft spirits industry to Crypto and Crypto to the craft spirits industry.

Roadmap / Timeline

Phase 1 - Base website & testnet of MVP smart contract Our phase 1 starts with Gitcoin grants round 14 to develop a Minimum Viable Product. The goals in this phase are to develop a basic smart contract that will allow for the representation of real spirits in the digital world; develop a robust and informative website; create a communication pathway for those interested while engaging with our audience; and to increase the awareness of the project. Phase 2 - Testing and development of webapp & smart contract integration Phase 3 - MVP mainnet release (alpha/beta version) with webapp testing Phase 4 - Version 1 development

Potential Use Cases

Real Whiskey that is represented as a utility NFT that is redeemable IRL. Virtual bars & distilleries in Decentraland, Sandbox, etc. Bonding of whiskey barrels - Loans backed by actual whiskey barrels; earn yield from whiskey. Revenue share - Invest in new barrels and earn a return with future sales and share in growth of the distillery and the industry. Collateral backing. Developing a new stable coin? Why not back the coin with real whiskey and spirits? Event passes. NFT Cocktails - Redeemable IRL. Whiskey clubs. Dare I say a DistilleryDAO or an entire distillery owned by a DAO. The list goes on…
